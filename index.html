My Motivation

My family has always been my motivation for just the simple fact that they are willing to support my dreams however unrealistic it may seem at the time.

On my own part, I intend to go way beyond their expectation. The thought of how vast the knowledge of this world is and how much we can accomplish when we tap into that knowledge, keeps me going.

However difficult any phase of my growth and development may be, I still try to push through when i think of these!

My Goals:

  • Acquire the skills and knowledge I need to thrive in the world of technology.

  • Make a significant mark in the tech industry and working to ensure growth in Africa and the world at large.

  • I also aspire to be a role model for future generation of African software engineers that strongly believe in their dreams whatever the odds may be.
